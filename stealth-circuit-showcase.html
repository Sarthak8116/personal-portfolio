01 The Vision

What we are building

Logic circuit design today is manual, error-prone, and slow. Engineers work with tools built decades ago, iterating through design-simulate-verify cycles that take weeks. We are building the AI layer that collapses that cycle.

01

AI-Native Design

Not a copilot bolted onto legacy tools. A ground-up rethinking of how logic circuits are specified, generated, and verified using modern AI. The design interface understands intent, not just syntax.

02

Higher-Level Abstraction

Engineers describe what the circuit should do at a behavioral level. The system handles the translation to gate-level implementation, optimization, and timing closure automatically.

03

Verification Built In

Every generated design comes with formal verification and simulation testbenches. Correctness is not an afterthought; it is part of the generation process itself.

04

Iteration in Minutes

Design cycles that take weeks compressed to minutes. Explore architectural tradeoffs, compare implementations, and converge on optimal designs at the speed of thought.
